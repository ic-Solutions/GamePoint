Our Cause

Rotary International's Mission

Providing Clean Water, Sanitation, and Hygiene

Providing everyone with potable water and good sanitation has always been one of Rotary International's key areas of focus.
As rotaractors, we're doing our part. This year's proceeds from Game Point are entirely being pledged towards improving potable water accesibility and sanitation to nearby locations. Given the intense temperatures that the city is facing, this is definitely the need of the hour.
Rotaract Club Of Koramangala believes in impactful projects that affect our people en masse, and Game Point has always been one of them. So join us for this edition of Game Point, and help us bring about the kind of change that matters.
